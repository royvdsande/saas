Privacy

Last updated: March 20, 2026.

FitFlow SaaS stores the minimum personal data required to provide authentication, premium access, account management, and operational support.

Data we use

  • Authentication data provided through Firebase Authentication.
  • Account and customer records stored in Firestore.
  • Billing and premium-status records created through Stripe-related Firestore collections.
  • Local browser storage for temporary account and premium-linking state.

Why we use it

We use this data to sign users in, manage premium access, complete checkout flows, and operate the app safely.
