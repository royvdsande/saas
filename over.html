About this boilerplate

This project is now positioned as a reusable SaaS starter instead of a product-specific experience. It gives you a clean dashboard shell, a landing page with sign-in, and generic marketing content you can adapt to your own product.

What is preserved

The Firebase authentication flow, admin controls, and premium billing infrastructure remain available so you can focus on building new product features rather than rebuilding the platform foundation.
